Monty's goat

01:00

Can you guess where Monty is?

A hide-and-seek loving goat named Monty Junior is behind one of these squares and you have to find him.

Choosing a square opens one(s) without Monty first. Then you can either stick with your choice or switch to another square.

If your final guess is right, you earn extra seconds based on how many times total you have clicked on the correct square and proceed to the next level. If you guess wrong, you just repeat the level.

You start out with 1 minute to play with Monty. Timer starts once you've made the first click.

Hint: Rock means that Monty is not in the side squares (sides = up, down, left or right).
