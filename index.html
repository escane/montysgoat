Monty's goat

How to play

There's a goat behind one random square each round you have to find. Picking a square opens random one without the goat. Then you can change your choice. Last pick reveals if you got it right.

If you guessed correctly, you get as many points as you clicked on the right square (up to 19), so the sooner you figure it out, the more points you get. But if you guess it wrong, you lose 5 points.

Then you can start again, until the time runs out. You have 1 minute.

Hint: Rock means that the goat is not in the side squares (sides = up, down, left or right).
